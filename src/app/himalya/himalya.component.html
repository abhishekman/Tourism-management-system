Great Himalayas

Rising from the Shivalik ranges and moving heigher into the mid-Himalyas, Greater Himalyas and beyond, into the trans-Himalayan vast expanse of western Tibet, Himachal Pardesh is a land of grand diversity.

Shelter by the ranges,rivers and their feeding tributary streams have carved out some of the most serene, scenic and feritile valleys.
