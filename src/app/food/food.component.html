Cuisine

Apples



Apples are the specialty of Himachal Pradesh fruits. The produce is fresh, juicy, and oozes with calm. They are the best variety you can find, search the globe if you must. The fruit grown on fields surrounding the Tobo monastery bears the finest of the finest. Apples are also the state fruit of Himachal Pradesh. The Shimla district produces about 70% of the total apple production of the state. The fruit has an economy of approximately Rs. 4000 crores. Also, there’s enough room for choice- of the 20 varieties of apples that India produces, 8 of them are grown here, in Himachal Pradesh! Best Season: Monsoon Season (Begins mid-July and ends in August) Where to Find: Shimla, Rohru, Kullu, Manali, Kotgarh and Kotkhai

Kale Aakhe




Kala hisalu is one of the main fruit of the local Himachal Pradesh fruits list known as the heartleaf raspberry. This delicious fruit can be found on the Himalayan foothills and is usually grown in the wild. They grow at elevations of 1,500-3,300 meters above sea level and in shrubs or bushy trees. Their color ranges from deep purple to maroon- this is based on how mature they are. It is widely grown and consumed in great amounts all over Himachal Pradesh locals and tastes sweet and sour. Best Season: Summer Season (April to June) Where to Find: Wild forest region of Himachal Pradesh

Anjeer



There is no need to mention how healthy anjeers or figs are, especially minerals, sugars, minerals, and fibers. Also, they are rich in potassium, calcium, magnesium, and iron. They usually taste sweet with hints of sour and are dried and pickled too. In some region of Himachal anjeer is called as a Himachali name which is “Dhude”.
